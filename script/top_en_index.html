The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a global consensus aimed at solving global challenges and achieving global sustainable development. The SDGs have a total of 17 goals, including poverty eradication, zero hunger, health and well-being, gender equality, clean water and sanitation, responsible production and consumption, climate action, life on land, peace and justice, partnership, etc.
